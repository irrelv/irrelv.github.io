Deckbuilder

This is a short bullet hell game with deckbuilder elements. The primary goal is to survive waves of increasingly powerful enemies while also upgrading a "deck" of bullets. Every few waves, the player will have the opportunity to add a bullet to their deck. Bullets are fired out in a random order, as if they were in a deck of cards that is shuffled every time the cannon reloads.

There are 7 zones of difficulty, each with a varying amount of waves. When the player has cleared a zone, they can add a powerful passive relic to their arsenal instead of a bullet. These relics give a passive buff and allow for interesting synergies or open new gameplay paths.

The game uses a 4-element status system where different types of bullets inflict different effects on enemies. Building a deck around these elements is important to maximize damage output. The status effects are explained in detail in the ingame instructions menu.

The average length of a game is 20 minutes. There are a total of 20 different types of bullets and 25 different relics available to the player.

The game uses free art assets provided by the talented people at https://game-icons.net/.
